AI Translator Privacy Policy

At AI Translator: Voice & Photo, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, protect, and handle your information when you use our mobile application ("AI Translator," "the App," "our App") on iOS devices. By using AI Translator, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Quick Summary: AI Translator is designed with privacy-first principles. We process translations locally on your device whenever possible. Your conversations, photos, and translations remain private. We do not sell your data to third parties. Offline mode ensures complete privacy with zero data transmission. We use Google AdMob for advertising and support in-app purchases through Apple's App Store.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide Directly

When you use AI Translator, you may provide certain information directly to us:

  • Voice Input: When you use voice translation features, your voice audio is temporarily processed to convert speech to text and translate it. Voice data is processed locally on your device when using offline mode, or sent to our translation servers for online translation.
  • Text Input: Text you type for translation is processed to provide translation results. Translation history is saved locally on your device.
  • Photo/Camera Input: When you use the photo translator feature, images you capture or upload are analyzed to extract and translate text. Photos are processed temporarily and are not stored on our servers unless you explicitly save them.
  • Translation History: Your translation history is stored locally on your device to provide quick access to past translations. You can delete this history at any time.
  • Language Preferences: Your preferred languages and settings are stored to personalize your experience.
  • Support Communications: When you contact us at engr.salmankh.77@gmail.com, we collect your email address and the content of your message to provide support.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

To improve the App's functionality and user experience, we automatically collect certain information:

  • Device Information: We collect device model, iOS version, unique device identifiers, device language settings, and network information.
  • Usage Data: We collect information about how you interact with the App, including features used (voice, photo, text translation), language pairs selected, translation frequency, and app performance metrics.
  • Crash Reports and Diagnostics: We collect crash reports and error logs to identify and fix technical issues. These reports may include device state information at the time of the crash but do not include your translation content.
  • Analytics Data: We use analytics tools to understand app usage patterns, popular features, and areas for improvement. This data is aggregated and anonymized.
  • Location Data (Optional): If you grant location permission, we may use your approximate location to suggest relevant languages or improve translation accuracy for location-specific terms. This is entirely optional.
  • Advertising Data: Google AdMob collects advertising-related data including your device advertising identifier (IDFA), IP address, and general interest data to serve relevant ads.

1.3 Permissions We Request

AI Translator requests the following permissions to function properly. You can manage these permissions in your device settings:

  • Microphone Permission: Required for voice translation features. Your voice is converted to text for translation purposes only.
  • Camera Permission: Required for photo translator features to capture images of text for translation.
  • Photo Library Permission: Required to access and translate text from photos you've already taken.
  • Speech Recognition: Required to convert your spoken words into text for translation.
  • Network Permission: Required for online translation when offline mode is not enabled.
  • Notification Permission: To send you alerts about translation completion, app updates, and optional tips.
  • Location Permission (Optional): For location-based language suggestions and improved translation accuracy.
  • Ad Privacy and SKAdNetwork: Allows Apple and Google to measure ad performance and personalize ads based on your interests.

3. Data Processing and Translation Privacy

3.1 Local Processing (Offline Mode)

AI Translator prioritizes local, on-device processing for maximum privacy:

  • Offline Translation: When you download language packs, all translations occur directly on your device without internet connection. Your voice, text, and photos never leave your device in offline mode.
  • Translation History: All translation history is stored locally on your device and never uploaded to our servers.
  • Voice Processing: In offline mode, voice recognition and translation happen entirely on your device.
  • Photo Analysis: Text extraction from photos is performed locally when using offline language packs.

3.2 Online Processing

When using online translation features (without downloaded language packs):

  • Text, voice audio, and photo data are temporarily sent to our secure translation servers
  • Data is transmitted using encrypted HTTPS connections
  • Translation requests are processed and immediately deleted after delivery of results
  • We do not store your translation content on our servers
  • Voice recordings are converted to text and the audio is immediately discarded

3.3 AI Translation Models

We use advanced AI translation models to provide accurate translations. These models are trained on publicly available multilingual datasets and do not include your personal translation data. Your translations are not used to train or improve our AI models without your explicit consent.

3.4 Data Retention

  • Translation Content: Not stored on our servers. All content is processed transiently and immediately discarded after translation delivery.
  • Translation History: Stored locally on your device until you delete it. You have complete control over your history.
  • Usage Analytics: Aggregated and anonymized usage data is retained for up to 24 months for improvement purposes.
  • Crash Reports: Retained for up to 12 months to identify and fix recurring issues.
  • Support Communications: Retained for up to 36 months for customer service purposes.
  • Ad Performance Data: Retained by Google AdMob per their privacy policies for campaign measurement and optimization.
